Services!



Public Awareness Campaigns

We develop campaigns, using media to highlight the "rarest of our rare" species, such as the Tara iti (New Zealand fairy tern).

Advocacy and Lobbying

We act as a "voice for nature" by lobbying for stronger protections for land, freshwater, and marine habitats, such as campaigning for the protection of Maui and Hector's dolphins.

Scientific Research & Monitoring

We use digital technological programs for wildlife monitoring (e.g., Wildlife.ai Trust) or commissioning ecological reports on species, such as the Kea.

Wildlife Tourism and Advocacy

We operate as a commercial entity that uses tours to educate the public, while funding conservation activities, such as Willowbank Wildlife Reserve's kiwi breeding programs.

Fundraising Initiatives

We organise events or sell merchandise to raise funds for targeted conservation projects (e.g., Endangered Species Foundation).
